# Disclaimer

## Intended Use

ClinLang is a **personal shorthand and templating tool for clinicians' own notes**. It is designed for one purpose only: helping a clinician transcribe and format their own observations more quickly. It expands user-defined abbreviations, organises text into SOAP / Markdown / JSON layouts, and stores notes as local files.

## Not a Medical Device

ClinLang is **not** a medical device under any jurisdiction, including but not limited to:

- United States — U.S. Food and Drug Administration (FDA)

ClinLang has not been evaluated or cleared by any regulatory authority. The software performs **transcription and formatting only**.

## What ClinLang Does Not Do

ClinLang does **not** provide, and must **not** be used as, any of the following:

- **Diagnosis** of any condition, disease, or symptom.
- **Treatment recommendations**, including drug selection, dosing, route, frequency, or duration.
- **Clinical decision support** of any kind.
- **Drug-drug interaction**, drug-allergy, or drug-disease checking.
- **Dose calculation**, maximum-dose checking, or renal/hepatic dose adjustment.
- **Risk scoring** (e.g. Wells, CHA₂DS₂-VASc, qSOFA, MELD, NEWS2, APACHE, GCS-based prognosis).
- **Imaging or signal interpretation**.
- **Triage** or any prioritisation of clinical urgency.
- **Alerts** for clinically critical states.

Any numeric range markers shown by ClinLang are **user-configurable transcription aids only**, derived from values the user themselves enters or imports. They are not clinical recommendations. The clinician supplies all clinical content and interpretation.
