    ClinLang: Fast, Shorthand Clinical Documentation

    Welcome to ClinLang! This tool is designed by and for clinicians to dramatically speed up daily charting, admission notes, and ward rounds.

    Instead of typing long, repetitive paragraphs in your electronic medical records (EMR) or hospital notes, you write in a natural, quick shorthand. ClinLang instantly translates your shorthand into a formal, structured SOAP (Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan) formatted note.

    [!IMPORTANT] Clinical Disclaimer ClinLang is a personal shorthand translation and note-taking tool. It is not a medical device. It does not provide clinical decision support, diagnostic assistance, dosing recommendations, or treatment guidelines. All clinical judgments, interpretations, and choices remain the sole responsibility of the clinician. See DISCLAIMER.md at the repository root.

    ⚡ The ClinLang Philosophy

    Keep your fingers on the keyboard and write notes at the speed of a patient presentation.

    What You Type What ClinLang Generates
    pt 58M wt82 Patient: 58Y/M | Weight: 82 kg
    cc chest pain for 1h Chief Complaint: chest pain for 1 hour
    sx pain+++ sob++ Symptoms: pain (very severe); sob (severe)
    vitals bp160/100 hr98 BP: 160/100 mmHg | HR: 98 bpm
    ix ecg:stemi trop+ Imaging: ECG stemi | Labs: TROP +
    rx aspirin 300mg stat po Plan: ▸ Rx: Aspirin 300mg Orally, Immediately
